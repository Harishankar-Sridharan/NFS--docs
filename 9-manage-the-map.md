# Nomadia Field Service

## **9. Manage the Map** 

The Manage Map feature helps you efficiently search and navigate locations. You can use address search or interact directly with the map to find tasks, appointments, and points of interest. 

|**Feature**|**Description**|
|---|---|
|Search Location|Locate specific locations by entering an address|
|Navigate Map|Use the map interface to zoom in, pan, and navigate around<br>areas of interest.|
|Locate Appointments|Define and search for tasks or locations within a specified<br>geographic area.|
|Filter Results|View and navigate directly to task or appointment locations on<br>the map.|



.
