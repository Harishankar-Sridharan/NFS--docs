# Nomadia Field Service

## **11. Glossary** 

   - **Agenda** : A detailed schedule of tasks and appointments for field technicians. 

   - **Resource** : Any entity, such as a technician or equipment, allocated to perform tasks. 

   - **Route Sheet** : A document outlining the optimal travel path for field operations. 

- **Task Assignment** : The process of allocating specific tasks to resources based on 

- availability and skill. 

   - **Planner View** : A customizable interface showing schedules and tasks for easy planning. 

   - **Unavailability** : Periods during which resources are not available for task assignment. 

   - **Appointment Panel** : A feature that allows users to view, edit, and manage scheduled appointments efficiently. 

   - **Customer Profile** : A record containing detailed information about a customer, such as name, address, and contact details. 

   - **Drag-and-Drop** : A user interaction method to move tasks or items within the interface by clicking, holding, and moving them to a new location. 

- **Filters** : Tools used to narrow down search results by applying specific criteria like 

- date range, job type, or status. 

- **Geographic Optimization** : The process of planning routes or tasks to minimize 

- travel distance and time. 

- **Intervention Types** : Categories of service or tasks performed during a customer 

- appointment (e.g., installation, maintenance, repair). 

   - **Map Integration** : The ability to display and interact with locations and routes directly on a digital map. 

- **Notification Panel** : A centralized area to view system alerts, unread messages, and 

- other updates. 

- **Resource Vehicle Tracking** : A feature to monitor the current location of field 

- vehicles in real-time. 

- **Schedule Freeze** : A feature that temporarily locks the current planning to prevent 

- unintended modifications. 

   - **Task Status** : Indicates the progress or state of a task, such as "Pending," "In
