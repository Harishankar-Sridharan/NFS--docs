### **7.4. Manage Opening Hours** 

The Manage Opening Hours feature allows you to configure and customize business or service operation hours. You can set time zones, define opening and closing times, and adjust schedules as needed. 

|**Feature**|**Description**|
|---|---|
|Set Working Hours|Define daily opening and closing times.|
|Configure Time Zones|Adjust time settings based on location.|
|Modify Schedules|Update hours based on operational needs.|
|Apply Changes|Save and implement schedule adjustments.|



**Confidential** 

**NFS – Planning Module User Guide** 

Page **45** of **76**

#### **7.4.1. Edit Opening Hours** 

1. Click on **Planning** in the menu. 

2. Open the **Customers** Dropdown list 

3. Select **Search for Customers** . 

4. Select the customer whose details you want to update 

5. Navigate to the **Timetable section** 

6. Select the **Time Zone** and adjust the **Opening Hours** as needed.
