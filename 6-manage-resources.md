# Nomadia Field Service

## **6. Manage Resources** 

The Manage Resources feature optimizes resource allocation by efficiently assigning and tracking personnel, vehicles, and equipment. It ensures smooth operations by managing availability and handling unavailability periods effectively. 

|**Feature**|**Description**|
|---|---|
|Personnel|Assign tasks to field personnel based on their|
|Management|availability and skill set.|
|Vehicle Allocation|Allocate vehicles efficiently for scheduled<br>tasks and monitor their usage.|
|Equipment Tracking|Track the availability and use of essential tools<br>and equipment.|
|Unavailability|Manage periods of unavailability to prevent|
|Handling|scheduling conflicts.|

### **6.2. Add a Job to a Resource** 

Temporary Jobs refer to short-term or ad hoc assignments that are not part of the standard planning schedule. These jobs are typically created to address urgent tasks, last-minute requests, or temporary work that does not follow a predefined workflow. Users can access Temporary Jobs through the Planning Menu, where they can view, assign, and manage these tasks efficiently. Unlike regular jobs, which are planned, temporary jobs allow for quick adaptability to operational needs. 

1. Click on **Planning** Menu. 

2. Open the **Planning** Dropdown List. 

3. Click on **Temporary Jobs** 


![](userguide_images/userguide.pdf-0023-07.png)


4. Enter the required details: 

   **1. Mobile Resources** 

   **2. Job** 

   **3. From** and **to** dates 

5. Click **Add a Job.**

### **6.4. Manage Resource Planning** 

The Resource Planning feature in the Nomadia Field Service Planning Module provides a centralized platform for managing and optimizing the schedules of field resources, ensuring efficient task allocation and seamless coordination.
